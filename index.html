From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders.

Min-Tech Ethiopia is a private company that processes and distributes non-metallic fillers. Its founders have experience in mineral processing, and the company benefits from the expertise of its foreign partner, Mazen Abu-Hijleh. Abu-Hijleh owns the largest filler and gypsum processing plant in North Sudan, offering an opportunity for knowledge sharing.

About our production line

To ensure the highest level of quality, our minerals undergo a rigorous processing method using state-of-the-art machinery capable of producing up to 3 tons per hour. Our advanced processing techniques ensure that the mineral fillers meet or exceed industry standards, ensuring the optimal performance and quality for our clients' applications.

Once the processing is complete, the minerals are carefully packaged in a variety of sizes to meet the specific requirements of our clients. Our packaging options are designed to offer maximum flexibility, with the ability to package the materials in bags, super sacks, or bulk shipments. The packaged materials are then safely stored in our spacious warehouses, waiting to be shipped to our clients.
