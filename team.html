Our Team

At Min-Tech Ethiopia, we value our team as the cornerstone of our success. With 108 individuals comprising our workforce, including permanent and contract staff, we prioritize efficient manpower allocation to drive our operations. Our team consists of 43 highly skilled permanent employees and 65 talented casual laborers, collectively contributing to the smooth functioning of our operations.

As we continue to experience growth and increasing demand for our products, we are committed to expanding our team accordingly. In order to ensure our production facilities operate at full capacity and meet the needs of our valued customers, we anticipate employing approximately 223 employees in the near future. By strategically increasing our workforce, we will be able to enhance our production capabilities and maintain the highest standards of quality and efficiency in our operations.
