About us

Processing excellence, delivering quality.

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders.

Min-Tech Ethiopia plc, established in May 2002 by two Ethiopian and one French national shareholders, is a privately owned company involved in the processing and distribution of non-metallic fillers in Ethiopia. The company's founders have extensive experience in mineral processing and distribution. What sets Min-Tech Ethiopia plc apart is the expertise of its foreign partner, Mr. Mazen Abu-Hijleh, who has over 30 years of experience in mineral processing.

Mr. Abu-Hijleh is currently the sales and after-sales technical service representative for Hosokawa Micron Group, one of the largest mineral processing equipment manufacturers in Germany, for the Middle East and North Africa region. He also owns the largest filler and gypsum processing plant, named AL Qudas Factory for Gypsum and Carbonate, in North Sudan, which provides an excellent opportunity for both companies to share expertise and knowledge due to its close proximity.

About our production line

To ensure the highest level of quality, our minerals undergo a rigorous processing method using state-of-the-art machinery capable of producing up to 3 tons per hour. Our advanced processing techniques ensure that the mineral fillers meet or exceed industry standards, ensuring the optimal performance and quality for our clients' applications.

Once the processing is complete, the minerals are carefully packaged in a variety of sizes to meet the specific requirements of our clients. Our packaging options are designed to offer maximum flexibility, with the ability to package the materials in bags, super sacks, or bulk shipments. The packaged materials are then safely stored in our spacious warehouses, waiting to be shipped to our clients.
